About ARTIFACT

ARTIFACT’s mission is to educate designers on emerging practices for cross-device web design and to foster a sense of cooperation in the community. We provide as many practical takeaways as possible, topped off with a layer of inspiration. The conference features an all-star line-up of leaders in the mobile design field. Come join us to discuss:

Responsive Workflow • Modern Deliverables • Tools and Technologies • Case Studies • Design Strategy • The Business of Design • A Glimpse of the Future
