ABOUT ARTIFACT

ARTIFACT's mission is to provide practical how-to information about the design process, topped off with a layer of inspiration—all delivered by leaders in our field.

Come learn about responsive workflows, prototyping strategies, tools, and technologies as well as tips for communicating effectively with your clients and stakeholders.

The two-day conference is followed by a day of workshops.
